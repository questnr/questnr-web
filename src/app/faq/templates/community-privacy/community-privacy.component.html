Questnr FAQ - Community Privacy
Community Privacy features help you to add restrictions on community content.
Public Community
All the pending community join requests will be approved automatically. All the community members and community posts will be visible to the public. Every Questnr user can join the community without admin's approval.
Private Community
All the requests has to be approved by the admin manually. Community members and community posts will be visible only to the joined community members. The community will not be displayed in the trending community section.
